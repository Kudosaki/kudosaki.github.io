Choose your path, refine your playstyle, and become stronger through real PvP.

Frequently Asked Questions

❓ What is Vaelithra?

Vaelithra is a class-based PvP Minecraft server where your power evolves through combat. Each class is tied to a unique weapon with abilities that grow as you fight.

❓ How do classes work?

Each class is represented by a signature weapon. When you equip a weapon, you gain access to its abilities and playstyle. Switching weapons allows you to switch classes.

❓ How do I get stronger?

You earn kills in PvP to unlock new abilities and upgrades. Progression is tied directly to combat, not grinding or pay-to-win systems.

❓ Is Vaelithra pay-to-win?

No. Vaelithra is designed to be completely skill-based. Purchases are cosmetic or convenience only and do not give combat advantages.

❓ How do I start playing?

Join the server using the IP (play.vaelithra.com), choose your class weapon, and start fighting. As you gain kills, you will unlock new abilities and improve your class.

❓ Is it beginner friendly?

Yes. The system is easy to learn but has depth for advanced players. You can quickly pick a class and start playing, then improve over time.
